## App Name
PatientBird Dictionary

## Subtitle
Minimal offline dictionary

## Description
A simple, private dictionary for readers.

Look up words while reading physical books. Type a word, get the definition. That's it.

• 100% offline - works without internet
• 100% private - no data leaves your device
• 100,000+ words (Webster's 1913 Dictionary)
• Light and dark mode
• Choose your font: sans, serif, or mono
• Recent searches for quick access

No ads. No tracking. No account required.
