PatientBird Dictionary

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PatientBird Dictionary is an offline dictionary app. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

What we don't do

  • We don't collect your search queries
  • We don't track how you use the app
  • We don't use analytics
  • We don't have accounts or logins
  • We don't send any data to servers

What stays on your device

The app saves your display preferences (dark mode, font choice) locally on your device using iOS settings storage. This data never leaves your phone.

External links

If you tap "Wikipedia" or "Search Web" for a word not found in our dictionary, you'll be taken to those websites. Their privacy policies apply once you leave our app.

Dictionary data

Our definitions come from Wiktionary and are stored entirely within the app. No internet connection is required to look up words.
